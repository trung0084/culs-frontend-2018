About Us

Coffeeforall shop & gallery offers you a unique cozy warm coffee house environment unlike any other in Prague. We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee( although you are more than welcome to do that). We are a place where you can sit down and enjoy your coffee. If you want to work, we have created a seating specially for the comfort of the people that would like to spend some time working while sipping their cup of coffee. If you want to rest/ relax, we have created a cozy soft- seating corner in front of a beautifully designed architecture of coffee origin. We offer a delicious variety of coffee from Europe and Africa. And you can complete your coffee with our delicious sweet treats by our own baker. We are looking forward to serving you at coffeeforall shop and gallery.
